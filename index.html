⚡ Energy

Footstep-Generated Electricity

Piezoelectric crystals compress under pressure and release a small electric charge. When embedded in floor tiles, every footstep generates ~5–8 milliwatts of electricity. In a busy station with 10,000 daily footsteps per tile, the energy adds up significantly!
